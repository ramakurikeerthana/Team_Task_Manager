Features

Nothing it doesn't.

Kanban & List Views
Switch between kanban boards and list views instantly. Your team, your workflow, your choice.
Real-time Collaboration
See who's working on what, right now. Assignments, comments, and updates live across your team.
Progress Analytics
Track velocity, spot blockers early, and ship sprints on time. Data that actually helps teams improve.
